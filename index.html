Our Story

Craft passed
hand to hand

पीढ़ी दर पीढ़ी की कारीगरी

Seema Meena started this shop in Veena Nagar with one simple idea — every woman should get clothes stitched exactly to her measurements. No adjustments, no guesswork. Just her size, her fit.

We never rush. The fabric is placed on the body first, pinned carefully, checked properly — and only then the scissors come out. No matter how urgent the order is, this step never gets skipped.

👩
Seema Meena
Founder and Master Tailor

How It Works

Four simple steps

📏I
Drape and Measure

We take measurements directly on the body. No guessing from far away.

✏️II
Choose Design

Fabric, embroidery, cut, style. Every choice is yours and we help you decide.

🧵III
Stitch and Finish

Cut and stitched by hand. A trial fitting is done to make sure everything is perfect.

🎁IV
Delivery

Pressed, packed and handed over exactly as promised.
